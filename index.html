about

The season is short. Don't spend it searching.

You know what you want to do — ski a line, learn to climb, chase a river, spend a night in an alpine hut. But finding who runs it, when it's on, and whether there are spots left takes twenty open browser tabs and half an afternoon.

BackcountryFinder is the fix. Every guided backcountry experience — courses, heli trips, cat skiing, fly fishing, hunting, huts, tours — in one place. Search, compare, save your favourites, and book direct.

how it works

We partner with providers to surface their real-time availability. The price you see is the provider's price — no markups, no fees added to your booking. We simplify the search. You book the adventure.
