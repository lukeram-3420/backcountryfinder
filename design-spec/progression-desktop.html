The path

From your first day on real rock to leading a multipitch route in the Bugaboos. Five courses, building one skill at a time, designed to take you from gym climber to self-sufficient alpine climber.

1

Foundation

Intro to outdoor rock climbing

2 days · Squamish · From $395

● ○ ○ ○ ○ Beginner No prerequisites

What to bring

Climbing shoes — everything else provided.

Your first day on real rock. Spend two days at Smoke Bluffs in Squamish learning to move on granite, tie in safely, belay outdoors, and read a route. By the end you'll have completed multiple top-rope climbs and know whether outdoor climbing is for you.

2

Skill builder

Anchor building & top-rope setup

1 day · Squamish · From $245

● ● ○ ○ ○ Novice After: Step 1

What to bring

Harness, climbing shoes, helmet. Personal anchor system if you have one.

Build the gateway skill that separates gym climbers from outdoor climbers: setting up your own top-rope anchors. Spend a full day at Smoke Bluffs learning equalization, redundancy, and rope management on real rock, with hands-on practice tying off natural and bolted anchors.

Learn more about this course →
3-6 MONTHS PRACTICE
3

Lead climbing

Intro to sport lead climbing

2 days · Squamish · From $445

● ● ● ○ ○ Intermediate After: Steps 1-2

What to bring

Harness, climbing shoes, helmet.

Move from following to leading. Two days on Squamish sport routes learning clipping technique, fall management, and lead belaying. By the end you'll have led your first outdoor sport routes with a guide watching closely.

Learn more about this course →
3-6 MONTHS PRACTICE
4

Trad & multipitch

Intro to trad & multipitch climbing

3 days · Squamish · From $695

● ● ● ● ○ Advanced After: Steps 1-3

What to bring

Harness, climbing shoes, helmet, trad rack if you have one.

Place your own protection and link multiple pitches together. Three days learning gear placements, anchor construction at belay stations, route-finding, and rope management for multipitch climbing on Squamish granite.

Learn more about this course →
6-12 MONTHS PRACTICE
5

Capstone

Bugaboos alpine climbing camp

5 days · Bugaboos · From $2,895

● ● ● ● ● Expert After: Steps 1-4

What to bring

Full alpine kit. See course page for the complete list — MSAA can also help advise.

Five days in the Bugaboos applying everything you've learned. Glacier travel, multi-pitch granite, and alpine rock climbing on iconic spires. Pre-trip prep call with your guide to confirm objectives and fitness.
